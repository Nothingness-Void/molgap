# Archive-r06: Structural GPS Adapter G0 Audit

This route tested whether explicit ring and conjugation topology supplies a
credible missing 2D representation for routed-v4 B3LYP residuals. It stopped at
the no-training G0 gate: the worst Gap-error decile is depleted, rather than
enriched, in ring-atom, fused-ring, ring-bond, and conjugated-bond measures.

No adaptor was trained, no model version was allocated, and no sealed set was
read. The full pre-registration, row-level audit, and decision are in
`results/phase8/archive/archive-r06-structural-gps-adapter/`.
